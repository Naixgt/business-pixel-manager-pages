Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: August 28, 2025

Your Right to Data Deletion

Under various privacy regulations and Facebook's platform requirements, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data that we have collected and processed through our "Business Pixel Manager" application.

What Data We Store

Our application may store the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Basic Facebook profile information and permissions
  • Pixel Data: Facebook Pixel configurations and settings you've created
  • Business Data: Business Manager connections and advertising account details
  • Usage Data: How you've interacted with our application
  • Technical Data: Logs and system information for service operation

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Email Request (Recommended)

Send an email to our support team with the following information:

  • Subject Line: "Data Deletion Request - Business Pixel Manager"
  • Your Facebook User ID or Email: (to identify your account)
  • Specific Data to Delete: (e.g., "all my data", "pixel configurations only")
  • Reason for Deletion: (optional, but helpful for improvement)

Email Address: support@netology-code.github.io

Option 2: Facebook App Settings

You can also revoke our app's access to your data directly through Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings â†’ Apps and Websites
  2. Find "Business Pixel Manager" in the list
  3. Click "Remove" or "Revoke Access"
  4. Confirm the removal

Note: This will immediately stop our app from accessing your data, but may not delete already stored information.

What Happens After Your Request

Processing Timeline

  • Immediate: We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours
  • Within 7 days: We will begin processing your deletion request
  • Within 30 days: All requested data will be permanently deleted
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete

Data Deletion Process

When we process your deletion request, we will:

  1. Verify Your Identity: Confirm that you are the rightful owner of the data
  2. Identify Data Sources: Locate all systems and databases containing your information
  3. Execute Deletion: Permanently remove your data from our systems
  4. Update Facebook: Notify Facebook that we've deleted your data (if required)
  5. Provide Confirmation: Send you a detailed report of what was deleted

What Cannot Be Deleted

Some data may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Legal Requirements: Data we're legally obligated to retain
  • Service Operation: Minimal technical data needed for app functionality
  • Security Logs: Access logs required for security and fraud prevention
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymous, non-personal statistical information

After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to access our service
  • All your pixel configurations will be permanently removed
  • Your Facebook app permissions will be revoked
  • You can reinstall the app later if you change your mind
